Where Student Ideas Shine

Fostering Innovation in Higher Education

Welcome to Galaxy, your gateway to a world of student-led innovation in Indian higher education. We believe that knowledge should be shared, and innovation celebrated. Explore a treasure trove of projects, connect with peers, and embark on a journey of academic discovery like never before. Join us in shaping the future of education.

Centralized Knowledge Hub
Galaxy serves as a centralized repository of academic projects undertaken by students from universities and colleges across India. It collects, organizes, and stores project information, making it easily accessible to students, educators, and researchers.
Plagiarism Detection
Galaxy integrates robust plagiarism detection tools to ensure the integrity of research and promote originality among students. It scans projects for similarities with existing content to maintain academic honesty.
Peer Learning
Galaxy encourages peer learning by enabling students to explore and learn from the work of their fellow students. It allows users to access and review projects, providing valuable insights and inspiration.
Cross-University Collaboration
Galaxy acts as a bridge for cross-functional research and collaboration between universities and colleges. It facilitates connections among institutions, educators, and students, allowing for joint projects and research initiatives.
Student Showcase
Galaxy highlights exceptional student projects, recognizing the outstanding work of individuals and teams. It provides a platform for students to showcase their achievements to a broader audience.
