Where Student Ideas Shine

Fostering Innovation in Higher Education

Welcome to Galaxy, where student innovation takes center stage. We believe in the power of education and its potential to transform lives. Galaxy is a pioneering platform that bridges the gap between knowledge, innovation, and collaboration in Indian universities.

Mission :

Our mission is to empower students in Indian universities to unleash their creative potential by providing a platform for showcasing their innovative projects and facilitating cross-university collaboration.

Vision :

Our vision is a future where every student project contributes to a nationwide pool of knowledge, fostering a culture of innovation, learning, and excellence in Indian higher education.
